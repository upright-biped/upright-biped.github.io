It is the 5th most realistic flight simulator in the world. You have 57 planes to choose from. The bad part is that there crazy pilots and you have to pay money for the subscription and app. If you get the subscription for one year and the app, it will total up too about $65. I think that it is worth it and you should definitely get it. It can definitely help be a future pilot. You can become a pro. There are grades and servers.
